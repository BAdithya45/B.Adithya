AIESEC

AIESEC, a global non-governmental and not-for-profit organization, is driven by the dynamic leadership of young individuals. It offers transformative business development internships and affordable travel experiences, empowering youth for positive social impact. With a presence in 120+ countries and a membership of around 40,000, AIESEC holds official recognition from prestigious entities. It has non-governmental consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), operates as an independent arm of the UN DPI, collaborates closely with the UN's Office of the Secretary-General's Envoy on Youth, and is a proud member of ICMYO. Recognized by UNESCO, AIESEC's international headquarters in Montreal, Canada, serves as a hub for young leaders dedicated to making a meaningful global difference.
